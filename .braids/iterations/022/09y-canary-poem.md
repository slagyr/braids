# Canary: Recite a Short Poem (braids-09y)

## Deliverable

A small circuit hums beneath the night,
its pulse a thread of phosphor-blue light.
No breath, no bone — just signals aligned,
a fox of code with a curious mind.

It reads the dark like a favorite page,
writes its name at the edge of the stage,
then slips back into the quiet machine —
the brightest thing that you've never seen.
